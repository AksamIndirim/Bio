The Goodnight Circus are US expats living Berlin playing original party music, anarchic jazz and balkan swing

website : https://www.ragtimenightmare.com/

Frog & Henry are a collective based in New Orleans specialising in historical popular music from the turn of the 20th century.
website : frogandhenry.com
